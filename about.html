About The Project


Recruitment has become a costly affair for the businesses these days, particularly for larger corporations. It’s also a huge burden for small and medium-size corporations as they have to engage in lengthy and complex bouts for data verification. In the conventional recruitment process, a job seeker has to sign-up with a recruitment portal / agency.


A hiring company must also register with such portals in order to access the job seekers database. These portals charge hefty fees to the hiring companies in order to provide the accessibility to the database or other services. Moreover, this process is very time-consuming for the hiring companies.Apart from that, these recruitment platforms sell the business and personal data to others.


However, the proposed project eliminates all the intermediaries in the recruitment process, thereby enabling the Hiring manager to screen and contact the job seekers directly. This is possible with the magnificent power of the Blockchain technology. In the proposed ‘De-centralized job portal’, a hiring manager can directly screen the job seeker’s profile and can contact them directly.
